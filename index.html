Case Study : Google Lens vs. MobileNet

Yeti Rambler

Result: Google Lens wins

Finn Cushions from Simply Cushions
Google Lens: Finn Cushions

Result: Google Lens wins

Dyson OnTrac™ Headphones (CNC Copper)
Google Lens: Dyson OnTrac™ Headphones

Result: Google Lens wins

Nike Zoom Freak 4 "All Star"
Google Lens: Nike Zoom Freak 4

Result: Google Lens Wins


Google Lens predicted all four images more accurately, and is by far more accurate than MobileNet. Developed by Nathan Martin.
